Introduction

What this is

  • An illustrated and opinionated HowTo on setting up RedHat OpenShift Data Science (RHODS) environments

What this is NOT

This is NOT:

  • A replacement for either of

    • RHODS documentation

    • OpenShift documentation

  • A way to build production-grade environments

  • The best design for all situations

Timing

  • Going through these steps will take anywhere between 30 minutes and a few hours, depending on:

    • If you already have a Managed OpenShift available

    • Whether it’s your first time setting up RHODS

    • How much customization you will require
